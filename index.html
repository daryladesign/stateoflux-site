DARYL A - CREATIVE DIRECTION + DESIGN + DEVELOPMENT

ABOUT

I'm a London-based creative director.

I have extensive experience in all aspects of real-time production, with a specialisation in leading technically challenging audio-visual projects.

Over the last 10 years, I've collaborated with many teams and artists such as Billie Eilish, Daft Punk, Eminem, Snoop Dogg, Juice WRLD, Epic Games, Meta, Riot, Disney, Nike, Punchdrunk and more.

I enjoy design and direction, alongside hands-on development in Unreal, TouchDesigner, Houdini, MaxMSP, electronics & creative technologies.

Recent awards include: 3x Cannes Lions, an Emmy Nomination, 2x Webbys, 2x BIMA Awards, FoST, and GOLD at the AEAF2019.

Building on my background in VFX and on-set production, I also develop tools on the Unreal Marketplace to connect traditional filmmaking with real-time technologies.
